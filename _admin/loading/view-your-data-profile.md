After you upload a CSV file, it is available as a table in ThoughtSpot. Click **Data** in the top navigation bar and select your table, and then click **Profile**.

![]({{ site.baseurl }}/images/data_profile.png)

The data profile includes null values, min, max, average, and sum information
for each table column. This **Profile** view should help you get a better sense
of whatâ€™s there before searching on the data.
